Chicken Shawarma

Description

Chicken shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with spiced, marinated chicken cooked on a grill or rotisserie and served in pita bread with vegetables and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, turmeric, garlic powder)
  • Lemon juice
  • Pita bread
  • Vegetables (lettuce, tomato, onion)
  • Sauce (tahini or garlic sauce)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice
  2. Cook chicken on grill or pan
  3. Slice chicken
  4. Fill pita bread with chicken, vegetables, and sauce
  5. Serve
