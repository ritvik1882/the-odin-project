Pizza Margherita

Description

Pizza Margherita is a classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Simple, fresh, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  3. Add mozzarella cheese
  4. Bake until crust is golden
  5. Top with basil and drizzle olive oil
